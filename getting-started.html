Getting started

Beginning is simpler than you think.

Taking the first step toward therapy is often the hardest part. Here's what you can expect when you reach out.

The process

How to get started

01

Reach out

Call or email to introduce yourself and share a little about what's bringing you to therapy. There's no pressure — this first contact is simply a chance to connect and ask any questions you might have.

02

Schedule a consultation

We'll schedule a brief phone or video consultation so you can get a sense of my approach and ask questions. This is also a chance for me to understand your goals and make sure we're a good fit.

03

Complete intake paperwork

Once we've confirmed we'd like to work together, please fill out the Informed Consent Form.

04

Your first session

Our first session is a chance to get comfortable, share more about yourself, and begin setting goals. There's no agenda — we go at your pace. Sessions are available in-person at either Chicago location or via telehealth.

Every journey begins with a single step.

Insurance

Coverage & payment

Depending on your current health insurance provider or employee benefit plan, services may be covered in full or in part. Please contact your provider to verify how your plan compensates you for psychotherapy services.

I am generally able to accept most PPO insurances, including:

  • Aetna
  • BCBS
  • Cigna
  • Medicare
  • United Healthcare

Questions to ask your insurance provider:

  • Does my plan include mental health benefits?
  • Do I have a deductible, and have I met it?
  • Does my plan limit sessions per calendar year?
  • Do I need written approval from my primary care physician?

I accept cash, check, and all major credit cards.
