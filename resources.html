Mental health

Mental health resources

These are organizations, tools, and hotlines Dr. Chawla recommends to clients and the broader community. If you are in crisis, please reach out to a crisis line immediately.

Crisis support

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Free, confidential support for people in distress. Call or text 988 anytime, 24/7. Chat also available online.

988lifeline.org
LGBTQ+ support

The Trevor Project

Crisis intervention and suicide prevention for LGBTQ+ young people. TrevorLifeline: 1-866-488-7386.

thetrevorproject.org
LGBTQ+ affirming

PFLAG

Support, education, and advocacy for LGBTQ+ people and their families. Local chapters available nationwide.

pflag.org
South Asian mental health

South Asian Mental Health Initiative (SAMHIN)

Mental health awareness, advocacy, and provider directory for the South Asian community in North America.

samhin.org
Postpartum support

Postpartum Support International

Resources, helpline, and support groups for postpartum depression, anxiety, and perinatal mental health.

postpartum.net
Anxiety & OCD

Anxiety & Depression Association of America

Education, resources, and a therapist directory for anxiety, depression, OCD, PTSD, and related conditions.

adaa.org
Physical health

Physical health resources

Mental and physical health are deeply connected. These resources support the whole-person approach Dr. Chawla brings to her practice.

Sleep

American Academy of Sleep Medicine

Evidence-based information on sleep disorders, sleep hygiene, and finding accredited sleep centers.

sleepeducation.org
Chronic pain

American Chronic Pain Association

Support groups, tools, and resources for people living with chronic pain and their care partners.

theacpa.org
General wellness

MedlinePlus

Reliable, easy-to-understand health information from the U.S. National Library of Medicine. No ads, no fluff.

medlineplus.gov
