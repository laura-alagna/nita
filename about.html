About Dr. Chawla

A therapist who believes in your authentic self.

I'm a licensed clinical psychologist based in Chicago, helping individuals and couples move past what's holding them back — toward a life that feels genuinely their own.

My approach

Closing the gap between who you are and who you want to be

Change requires courage, but with professional guidance, you can develop the skills needed to reach your goals. My therapeutic toolkit includes evidence-based techniques such as effective communication strategies, mindfulness practices, relaxation methods, and cognitive restructuring to address negative thought patterns.

In couples therapy, I emphasize the development of healthy communication patterns while exploring the underlying dynamics that influence relationship health. I particularly enjoy supporting professionals experiencing anxiety and working with members of the LGBTQ+ community.

I have extensive professional experience working with individuals from the LGBTQ+ community and South Asian backgrounds, bringing genuine cultural understanding and sensitivity to every client relationship.

Clinical expertise

I have found clinical hypnotherapy to be highly effective for addressing chronic and acute pain conditions, weight management challenges, smoking cessation, stress reduction, sleep disturbances, and anxiety and panic disorders. My training in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and clinical hypnosis enables me to provide specialized support for clients experiencing various anxiety presentations and panic disorders.

What guides my work

Authenticity

Every person has an authentic self worth uncovering. Therapy is the process of clearing the way to it.

Inclusion

My practice welcomes people of all backgrounds, identities, orientations, and relationship structures — no exceptions.

Collaboration

You are the expert on your own life. My role is to be a skilled, supportive partner in the work you're ready to do.
