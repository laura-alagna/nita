About Dr. Chawla

Helping you close the gap between who you are and who you want to be

My mission is to help each client move closer to discovering and embracing their authentic self. I believe that anxiety and depression often arise when we struggle to know and accept who we truly are. Societal expectations and early childhood experiences can create barriers to genuine self-acceptance.

I have extensive experience working with individuals from the LGBTQ+ community and South Asian backgrounds — bringing cultural sensitivity and lived understanding to every session.
