New parents

Postpartum support

Becoming a parent is one of the most profound transitions in life — and one of the least talked-about emotional experiences. Whether you're navigating postpartum depression, anxiety, identity shifts, relationship changes, or simply the overwhelming reality of new parenthood, you don't have to do it alone.

I provide compassionate, evidence-based support for new and expecting parents of all backgrounds and family structures. LGBTQ+ families, single parents, adoptive parents, and parents navigating South Asian cultural expectations around parenthood are all warmly welcomed here.

What I can help with

Postpartum depression and anxiety · Baby blues that aren't lifting · Identity and role changes · Relationship strain after baby · Birth trauma · NICU stress · Navigating family pressure · Feeding and sleep exhaustion · Returning to work

Support is available for all parents — birthing and non-birthing, primary caregivers and partners. Telehealth sessions make it easy to access care from home during those early weeks and months.

Remote sessions

Telehealth

Telehealth makes quality therapy accessible no matter where you are in Illinois. Sessions take place over a secure, HIPAA-compliant video platform — same care, same connection, from wherever you feel most comfortable.

Whether you're a new parent at home, navigating a demanding schedule, or simply prefer the privacy of your own space, telehealth is a full-featured option — not a compromise.

Dr. Chawla is certified to practice telepsychology in 43 states that are part of The Psychology Interjurisdictional Compact. PSYPACT® is an interstate compact designed to facilitate the practice of telepsychology and the temporary in-person, face-to-face practice of psychology across state boundaries.

Group therapy

There is something uniquely powerful about healing in community. Group therapy brings together people navigating similar experiences, offering both professional guidance and peer connection that individual therapy can't replicate.

Groups are carefully curated and professionally facilitated. Hearing others' perspectives can unlock insight into your own life — while the shared experience itself becomes part of the healing.

Mind-body work

Clinical Hypnosis

Clinical hypnosis is a safe, evidence-supported technique that works with the subconscious mind to create positive change. Far from the stage-show stereotype, therapeutic hypnosis is a collaborative, focused state of relaxation and heightened awareness.

It works exceptionally well alongside talk therapy and is particularly effective for anxiety, chronic pain, sleep difficulties, and habit change.

Anxiety and stress reduction
Chronic pain management
Sleep and insomnia
Habits and behavioral change
